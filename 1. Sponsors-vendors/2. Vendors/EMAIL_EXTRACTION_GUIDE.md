## EMAIL EXTRACTION METHODS

### Method 1: Website Contact Pages
1. Visit business website
2. Look for:
   - `/contact` page
   - `/about` page
   - Footer section (often contains email)
   - Contact form (may reveal email on submit or in page source)
3. Check page source (Ctrl+U / Cmd+U) for email addresses in HTML

### Method 2: Google Business Listings
1. Search: "[Business Name] [City]"
2. Check Google Business profile
3. Look for email in business information
4. Check reviews for business responses (may include contact info)

### Method 3: Social Media Pages
1. For Facebook-only businesses:
   - Check Facebook page "About" section
   - Look for contact information
   - Check posts for email addresses
2. For businesses with Instagram:
   - Check bio for email
   - Check contact information

### Method 4: Yelp / Yellow Pages
1. Search business on Yelp
2. Check business profile for email
3. Check Yellow Pages listing

### Method 5: Email Pattern Guessing
For businesses with websites, try common patterns:
- info@[domain]
- contact@[domain]
- hello@[domain]
- admin@[domain]
- [businessname]@[domain]

**Note**: Always verify email addresses before adding to lead list.
