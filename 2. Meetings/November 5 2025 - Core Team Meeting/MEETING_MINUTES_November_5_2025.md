## 3.0 Key Decisions Made



### 3.1 Race Format & Timing



**Kids Race Timing - DECISION:**

- **Kids 1K Race:** 8:00 AM (BEFORE main 5K race)
- **Main 5K Race:** 8:00 AM (immediately following kids race)
- **Rationale:** Allows parents to run with kids, then participate in main race
- **Format:** Out-and-back course OR obstacle course with fun elements
- Follows Moon in June model (kids first, adults after)



**No 10K Option - DECISION:**

- Team decision to focus on 5K-only for inaugural year
- Strategic choice to simplify operations and logistics
- Build strong foundation with successful 5K event first
- Plan to expand to 10K in future years once established



**No Walker Category - DECISION:**

- 1.5-hour road closure window too tight
- May revisit in future years with longer route approval



---



### 3.2 Pricing Strategy



**Early Bird Extension - DECISION:**

- **Previous:** Expires November 10
- **New:** Extend to December 5 (Black Friday week)
- **Rationale:** Capitalize on Black Friday promotion mindset
- **Next Tier:** January 31 (next pricing tier)
- **Final Month:** June registrations may not include guaranteed shirt



---



### 3.3 Vendor Booth Pricing



**Decision:** $250 per booth (10x10 space)

- Significantly below market rate (typical: $400-500+)
- Subsidized to ensure strong vendor participation in Year 1
- Build relationships for future years
- Increase pricing in subsequent years
- Park rental from city is free (included in park booking)
