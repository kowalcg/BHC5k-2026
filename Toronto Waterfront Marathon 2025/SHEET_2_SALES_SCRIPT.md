# BRONTE HARBOUR CLASSIC 5K - SALES SCRIPT
**Quick Talking Points for Volunteers**

## THE 30-SECOND PITCH

*"Hi! Are you planning any races for 2026? We're launching the first-ever Bronte Harbour Classic 5K on Father's Day - it's a full-day festival in Oakville with live music, vendors, and activities. Premier pricing is only $45.82 until November 9th, then it goes up to $62+. Scan this QR code to lock in the best price!"*

## CONVERSATION STARTERS (Pick What Feels Natural!)

**Option 1 - Direct:**  
*"Are you running the marathon? Planning any 2026 races?"*

**Option 2 - Value:**  
*"Looking for a great race deal? $45 for a 5K - only until November 9th!"*

**Option 3 - Family:**  
*"Looking for a Father's Day activity? We have a 5K, Kids 1K, and all-day festival!"*

**Option 4 - Location:**  
*"Ever run by the lake in Oakville? Beautiful course - Premier pricing until Nov 9!"*

## QUICK RESPONSES TO OBJECTIONS

**"I need to think about it"**  
→ *"No problem! Scan this QR code so you have all the info. Premier pricing ends November 9th - still time but don't wait too long!"*

**"That's too far from me"**  
→ *"It's only 30 minutes west of Toronto! Beautiful lakeside venue. Perfect Father's Day trip for the family!"*

**"I'm not fast enough"**  
→ *"Perfect for all paces! It's a community celebration - run, jog, or walk. Flat, scenic course. Everyone's welcome!"*

**"What about my kids?"**  
→ *"We have a Kids 1K for ages 2-12! Only $29 until November 9th. They get a medal and festival access!"*

**"I'll check it out later"**  
→ *"Great! Just scan this QR code now. Premier pricing ends Nov 9 - after that it's $62+!"*

## KEY POINTS TO EMPHASIZE

✅ **First-ever inaugural event** - Be part of history  
✅ **Premier pricing ends Nov 9** - Best price ever, saves $43  
✅ **Father's Day festival** - Full-day experience, not just a race  
✅ **Beautiful Oakville location** - Lakeside, 30 min from Toronto  
✅ **Kids welcome** - Kids 1K for ages 2-12  
✅ **All paces welcome** - Community celebration  
✅ **Supports local charity** - Oakville Dads Charity

## BOOTH TIPS

**DO:**
- Stand up and smile when people approach
- Make eye contact and be friendly
- Point to QR codes - make it easy to scan
- Say "Premier pricing until November 9th"
- Listen and tailor your pitch to their interests

**DON'T:**
- Be pushy if they say no
- Block walkways
- Talk too fast
- Forget to mention the November 9th deadline

## THE CLOSE

*"Can I help you scan the QR code to register today? Premier pricing ends November 9th!"*

**REMEMBER:** Every conversation builds awareness! Make it easy for them to take action. Focus on Premier pricing urgency!
