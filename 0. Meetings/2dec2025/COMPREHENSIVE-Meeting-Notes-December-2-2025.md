- Makes race more exciting for all participants

- Kids' race: 10 minutes
- 5K race: ~70 minutes (accounting for slowest runners at 50 minutes)
- Lead finishers: ~25-30 minutes
- Total road closure: 3 hours (6:30 AM - 9:30 AM)
